Virtual Mouse Gesture List

1. Move cursor
   Show your hand normally and move your index finger.

2. Left click
   Touch thumb tip to index finger tip briefly.

3. Right click
   Touch thumb tip to middle finger tip briefly.

4. Drag
   Hold the left-click pinch longer.

5. Scroll
   Extend four fingers upward.

6. Draw toggle
   Touch thumb tip to ring finger tip.

7. Voice command
   Show only index and middle fingers up, with ring finger and pinky folded.
   Hold it steady for a short moment to start listening.

Supported voice commands
- open notepad
- open browser
- take screenshot
- capture screenshot
- mute volume
- maximize window
- clear canvas
